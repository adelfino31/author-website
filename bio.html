Audra Delfino released her debut novel, Brayan Island, in 2025. She has always been interested in writing books, ever since elementary school when her parents got their first computer and put it in the living room. Audra was eight years old writing romance and adventure stories on Microsoft Word. She was inspired for the idea of Brayan Island by Block Island, which is a small island off the coast of Rhode Island. Block Island is somewhere Audra has frequented many times with her family. She says there is magic in the air on Block Island. The air is full of love and adventure. Audra says that when she has gone to Block Island in the past, the characters had come to life in front of her and helped tell her the story of Brayan Island.

Audra has plans to continue writing the story of Brayan Island. A side character in Brayan will definitely be getting their own book. She's also always been interested in writing a fantasy novel or series, so hopefully that is to come in the future as well.

Audra has been inspired by many different novels and movies for her writing. As long as she's been a writer, she's also been an avid reader. Audra never leaves her house without a book tucked into her purse, because even if she's just going to work or to a friend's house, her motto is, "You never know when you're going to get a chance to read, and you don't want to be unprepared." One of her favorite series is Magnolia Parks, by Jessa Hastings, where she feels she has gotten a lot of inspiration from. Other inspiration for Brayan has come from authors such as Carley Fortune, Jenny Han, Christina Lauren, Abby Jimenez, Taylor Jenkins Reid, as well as many others.
